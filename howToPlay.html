HOW TO PLAY?

About The Game

Black Or Red is a game of debate, manipulations and lies.

Each player gets either black or red card.

You can choose who will be the villians in the game. Let's say that we choose the reds to be the villians.

The purpose of the blacks is to reveal all the reds, while the purpose of the reds is to eliminate all the blacks without being discovered.

The blacks wins when they succeed to reveal all the reds.

The reds wins when the number of the reds equal to the number of the blacks left in the game.

Start A New Game

First, set the setting of the game.
Choose your group name,
number of players, color of the villians
and number of villians.

Recommended ratio is 1 villians on every 3 "citizens".

When done, click "LET'S GO!".

Now invite your friends.
Each player should enter his name and press "PLAY!".

After getting your card, watch it discreetly, no one should know what you got.

Choose a narrator. Before the game start, the narrator asks from all the participants to close their eyes.

Then he asks for the villians to raise their head and watch each other.
After the villians watch each other, The narrator ask them to close their eyes again.

Now the narrator "wakes" everybody, and the game begin!

At the end of each round there's a vote, the player who gets the majority of the votes get eliminated.

Try not to be very suspicious,
Look for suspicious behaviour among others, lie, collaborate, deceive, argue,
and have a lot of fun!
